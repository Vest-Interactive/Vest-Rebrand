The Appalachian Regional
Healthcare Campaign

Appalachian Regional Healthcare offers state-of-the-art heart care in southeastern Kentucky, however many were driving hours to metropolitan hospitals unaware that life-saving treatment was just down the street.

Feature local residents whose lives were changed by ARH high-quality care and treatment close to family and home.

A 98% community awareness of quality healthcare close to home that helped to build additional hospital wings.
