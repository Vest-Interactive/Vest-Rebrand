Site Redesign and Branding Enhancement

Overview

Dr. Reddy came to Vest needing help. She was overspending on marketing efforts that were not getting her results. She needed answers to questions in the digital space but didn't know what to ask. What was happening to the traffic once it got to her page and why was nobody acting? That is where Vest stepped in and helped clarify the constraints and designed a plan that made sense and would show results.

Site Redesign for
Dr. Reddy Dental

Dr. Reddy's prior site was a template site with general dental information on it. Her website did not have the detail needed to stand out. And did not support mobile devices such as phones and tablets.

The interactive team developed a research plan, by continuously analyzing the UX (User Experience) of Dr. Reddy's current website and brand assets/logo. The new plan was tailored specifically for her needs and goals.

The interactive plan called for bold change, this was to drive leads through her web site by having more defined call to action elements placed throughout the site. The recommended change to deliver the leads required the site to be redesigned to support mobile web.

After the research comes the paper and pencils, first stop was the logo redesign.

Mockups & Design

The process for creating the new website for Dr. Reddy, involved concepting mockups that would demonstrate what the site would look and how it could function.

Iterating over wireframes and discussing the importance of different layouts helped Dr. Reddy understand the importance of design decisions and how they would impact the user experience on her site and drive leads to act.

The mockup wireframes are shared, feedback gets quickly collected, processed and merged into the design.

Development

Reddy-dental.com was designed mobile first and so the development involved laying out the site for both desktop, mobile phone and tablet use. The mobile first approach gives Dr. Reddy's site the needed change to display properly and drive leads.
